What’s the Consulting Championship?

The Consulting Championship 2025 is a flagship national competition developed by Aramco with Deloitte, EY, and Roland Berger. Student teams tackle real business cases, attend expert-led workshops, present to judges, and gain exposure to consulting careers.

Endorsed by all partner firms.

Who can participate

  • Must be an undergraduate student at a Saudi university
  • Teams of 3–4 students (one team leader)
  • Only the team leader needs to submit the application
  • All majors can apply

What you’ll do

  1. Learn from the firms

    Join live case delivery and debrief sessions led by Deloitte and Roland Berger.

  2. Work as a team

    Analyze the case, build your storyline, and prepare a crisp submission.

  3. Present to judges

    Selected teams pitch to an international panel with detailed feedback.
