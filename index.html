What’s the Consulting Championship?

The Consulting Championship 2025 is a flagship national competition developed by Aramco with Deloitte, EY, and Roland Berger. Student teams tackle real business cases, attend expert-led workshops, present to judges, and gain exposure to consulting careers.

Built on best practices from leading case competitions and datathons: clear stages, concise benefits, partner-balanced presentation.
